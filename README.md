# 🏥 MedVision – Electronic Medical Record (EMR) System

## 🚀 Overview

MedVision is a web-based **Electronic Medical Record (EMR)** system that allows centralized storage and management of patient health data.
It enables administrators to maintain medical records and provides quick access to patient information through a simple interface.

---

## 🎯 Problem

Traditional medical record systems are:

* Paper-based and hard to manage
* Difficult to retrieve during emergencies
* Prone to data loss and duplication

---

## 💡 Solution

MedVision provides a **digital platform** to:

* Store patient medical records securely
* Access patient data quickly
* Maintain organized health history

---

## ⚡ Key Features

* 🧑‍💼 **Admin Panel**
  Add, update, and manage patient records

* 📄 **Medical Records Management**
  Store reports, prescriptions, and history

* 🔍 **Patient Information Access**
  View essential medical details when needed

* 🗂 **Centralized Database**
  Structured storage of all patient data

---
